Got questions?
Common questions
Everything you need to know before you connect.
Is Aulert free to use?
Yes, completely free. No credit card, no subscription, no trial period. Connect your Google account and start getting notified immediately.
Can Aulert post or modify anything in my Classroom?
Absolutely not. We request read-only access scopes. Aulert can only observe — it cannot post, comment, submit, or delete anything on your behalf.
How fast are the notifications?
Aulert checks your Classroom every 5 minutes. Once it detects something new, the push notification arrives within seconds — so the maximum delay you'll ever experience is around 5 minutes.
Does it work for all my classes at once?
Yes. Once connected, Aulert monitors every active course in your Google Classroom account simultaneously. You can toggle individual classes off from the settings page.
What types of activity does Aulert detect?
Stream posts, teacher announcements, comments on your submitted work, new assignments, grade releases, material uploads, and deadline changes. If it appears in your Classroom, we'll catch it.
